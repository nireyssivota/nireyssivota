Safety

The sea is a joy when we respect it.

  • Wear a life jacket in choppy seas — children always.
  • Avoid shallows and rocks. Watch for swimmer buoys.
  • Meltemi/strong winds: stay on the leeward side of islets.
  • Phone charged, stay in touch with base.

You'll receive a detailed briefing before departure.
