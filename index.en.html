Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about boat rentals in Sivota.

Do I need a license to rent a boat in Sivota?

No license is required for boats up to 30hp — before departure you'll receive a short orientation and navigation instructions. A speedboat license is required for higher-powered boats.

What's included in the rental?

Pickup/drop-off, basic safety equipment and a briefing on routes, anchorages and points to watch out for. For per-boat details, see the fleet or just ask us.

How many people fit in the boat?

It depends on the model. On the “Fleet” page you'll see each boat's capacity, and we'll suggest the right one for your group.

How do I book / check availability?

Send us your date, group size, and whether you have a license (or want a skipper) and we'll suggest available options.

Is fuel included?

Fuel is usually charged separately, depending on use/route. We'll explain everything precisely before you head out.

Can I book a private cruise with a skipper?

Yes — ideal if you want a relaxed experience and local guidance to beaches, caves and small islands, without driving the boat yourself.

What should I bring?

Sunscreen, a hat, water, a towel and a light jacket for the late afternoon. For kids, we always recommend a life jacket.

What happens in strong winds or bad weather?

Safety comes first. If conditions aren't suitable, we'll suggest changing the time/date or taking an alternative route in more sheltered spots.
